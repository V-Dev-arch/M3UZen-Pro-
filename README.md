## 🎯 What is M3UZen Pro?

Tired of spending **hours in Visual Studio** just to edit a simple playlist? 

M3UZen Pro is here to change that. It's a lightning-fast, intuitive M3U playlist editor that lets you **add, delete, and reorganize channels instantly** — right where they belong. No coding. No complex tools. Just pure playlist zen.

## 📖 Usage Guide

1. **📂 Load** your M3U playlist file
2. **🔍 Browse** your channels in the clean grid layout
3. **✏️ Edit** channel names, URLs, and metadata with one click
4. **🗑️ Delete** unwanted channels instantly
5. **➕ Add** new channels exactly where you want them
6. **🔄 Reorder** by dragging and dropping
7. **💾 Export** your perfected playlist
